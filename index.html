About

Priya holds a PhD in Electrical Engineering from Texas A&M University, where her research applied Bayesian inference methods to genomic data — work published in venues including the Journal of Computational Biology. That background in rigorous statistical modeling now shows up in how she approaches production AI: measured, evaluation-first, and skeptical of anything that can't be tested.

She led teams building production AI systems and fine-tuned LLMs for education at Chegg, and now leads AI science teams at Intuit, where she builds personalized, agentic AI systems for finance. Her work integrates cutting-edge agentic workflows with robust evaluation systems — driving business impact while keeping safety, reliability, and compliance non-negotiable.

Priya is a strong advocate of responsible AI, mentors early-career AI scientists and engineers, and advises startups on AI strategy.
